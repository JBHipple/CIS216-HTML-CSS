22 million Americans suffer from some type of Obstructive Sleep Apnea. Could Sleep Apnea be preventing you from getting a quality night's sleep?

What is Sleep Apnea?

Sleep Apnea is a potentially serious sleep disorder in which breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep. Sleep Apnea can prevent you from entering restful sleep, causing many health problems as a result.

Sleep Apnea can lead to long term health effects, such as High Blood Pressure, Daytime Fatigue, Liver Problems, Narcolepsy, and can lead to surgical issues, especially involving anesthesia.
